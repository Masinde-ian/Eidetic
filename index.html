Behind The Lens

At Eidetic, we believe that the best photographs do more than capture how something looks, they capture how it felt. Whether it’s the quiet in-between moments at a wedding, the laughter of a family session, or the power of a striking brand image, our goal is to tell your story in an honest and beautiful way.

We specialize in weddings, portraits, events, or commercial work, blending a timeless aesthetic with a relaxed, thoughtful approach. From our first conversation to the final delivery, we’re committed to making the experience as enjoyable and meaningful as the photos themselves.

This passion for storytelling through photography is rooted in the vision of our founder, Nelson. With over 10 years behind the lens, Nelson brings a deep appreciation for connection, creativity, and the details that make each session unique. What started as a personal love for capturing moments has grown into a full-time mission to help others preserve theirs.

We’d love to get to know you, and help tell your story, one frame at a time.
